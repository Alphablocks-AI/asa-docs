SOPs, FAQs, and product notes often live in Notion before they reach your storefront. This guide connects Notion to Asa and trains only the pages you choose.

<Steps titleSize="h4">

<Step icon="plug" title="Connect your Notion account">

Navigate to **Add Content** in the sidebar, open the **Apps** tab, and click **Connect** on the **Notion** card.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/add-content-section.png" alt="Add Content Apps tab showing integration cards including Notion" />
</Frame>

A Notion sign-in window opens. Sign in with the workspace account that owns the pages you want to train, then approve Asa’s access request.

<Note>
Authorizing Notion does not train Asa on your content yet. You choose which pages to train in the next step.
</Note>

</Step>

<Step icon="file-lines" title="Choose pages and train">

After OAuth completes, the **Allow Asa to access these Notion pages** dialog opens automatically.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/notion-pages-dialog.png" alt="Notion page selection dialog with checkboxes and Proceed button" />
</Frame>

Select the pages you want Asa to learn from, then click **Proceed**. Training usually takes 1–2 minutes depending on how much content you selected.

<Check>
Go to **Manage Content** and confirm your Notion sources appear with a **Trained** status.
</Check>

</Step>

</Steps>

## Keep content up to date

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Add more pages | On the Notion card, open the **Connected** menu and choose **Manage integration** to open the page picker again. |
| Retrain after edits | Update the page in Notion, then retrain from **Manage Content** so Asa uses the latest version. |
