Follow these steps to connect Asa to your **Klaviyo** account using their official app flow.\
This allows Asa to access Klaviyo data and use it to power richer, more personalized conversations with your customers.

### Step 1: Open Klaviyo in Asa

- In your **Asa dashboard**, go to **Add Content**.
- Open the **Integrations** tab.
- Find **Klaviyo** in the list of integrations.
- Click **Connect** under **Klaviyo**.

![Add Content](/images/add-content.png)

### Step 2: Authorize Asa in Klaviyo

- When you click **Connect**, Asa will open the Klaviyo authorization flow in a new window or tab.
- If you’re not already signed in to Klaviyo, log in with your Klaviyo account.
- Review the permissions requested by the Asa integration.
- Click **Allow** or **Authorize** to grant access.

Once authorization is complete, you’ll be redirected back to Asa and the connection process will continue automatically.

![Klaviyo Auth](/images/klaviyo-auth.png)

### Step 3: Wait for connection to complete

- Asa will finish the connection in the background.
- This usually takes less than a minute.
- Once connected, the integration status in **Add Content → Integrations → Klaviyo** will display as **Connected**.

### Notes

- Make sure you’re authorizing the correct Klaviyo account and workspace that matches your Shopify or primary storefront.
- If the authorization window is blocked, check your browser’s popup blocker settings and try again.
- If you encounter any issues, contact us at **help@alphablocks.ai** — we’re happy to assist.
