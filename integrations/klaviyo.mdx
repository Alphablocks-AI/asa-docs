Shoppers trust answers backed by real reviews. Connect Klaviyo Reviews once and Asa can use your review data alongside your Shopify catalog — about two minutes end to end.

<Steps titleSize="h4">

<Step icon="plug" title="Start the connection in Asa">

Navigate to **Add Content** in the sidebar, open the **Apps** tab, and click **Connect** on the **Klaviyo Reviews** card.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/add-content-section.png" alt="Add Content Apps tab showing integration cards including Klaviyo Reviews" />
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step icon="shield-check" title="Authorize Asa in Klaviyo">

Klaviyo’s authorization window opens in a new tab.

- Sign in with the Klaviyo account tied to your store’s reviews.
- Review the permissions Asa requests.
- Click **Allow** or **Authorize** to grant access.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/klaviyo-auth.png" alt="Klaviyo OAuth authorization screen for Asa" />
</Frame>

When authorization completes, you return to Asa and the connection finishes automatically — usually in under a minute.

</Step>

<Step icon="circle-check" title="Confirm connected">

On the **Klaviyo Reviews** card, the status shows **Connected**.

<Note>
Only one reviews app can be connected at a time. If you already use Judge.me or Yotpo, disconnect that integration before connecting Klaviyo Reviews.
</Note>

</Step>

</Steps>

## Notes

- Authorize the Klaviyo account and workspace that match your Shopify store.
- If the authorization window does not open, check your browser’s popup blocker and try again.

If you need help, reach out at [help@alphablocks.ai](mailto:help@alphablocks.ai).
