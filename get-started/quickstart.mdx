---
title: "Get Live in Minutes"
description: "This guide gets you from creating a new account to Asa live on your storefront in minutes."
---

<Steps titleSize="h4">
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [asa.so](https://asa.so) and click **Sign In** in the top right. Create an account with your email and set a password. Complete the account verification steps. When you finish, you land in your Asa dashboard.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your content">

    Asa answers from what it knows. Go to **Add Content** in the sidebar.
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/add-content.png" alt="Add Content screen showing Apps, Files, Websites and Snippet tabs" />
    </Frame>

    **On Shopify?** Connect it first — it's your most valuable source. Products, pages, policies, and collections sync automatically.

    <Card title="How to connect Shopify" href="/integrations/shopify" arrow="true" icon="shopify" horizontal>
    Quick setup guide to connect Asa to your Shopify Store to add content
    </Card>

    **Everything else** — use the tabs across the top:

    | Tab      | What to add                                                          |
    | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
    | Apps     | Shopify, Notion, Google Drive, Judge.me, Yotpo, Klaviyo Reviews               |
    | Files    | PDFs, CSVs, Docs — pages, return policies, guides                    |
    | Websites | Any URL — Asa reads and learns from it                               |
    | Snippet  | Paste text directly — good for short policies or FAQs |

    <Tip>
    Start with products and policies. They answer the majority of shopper questions. Add everything else after you're live.
    </Tip>

    Upon connecting Asa to your third party apps and adding content to train from, navigate to **Manage Content** to check training status in real-time.

  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm your content is trained">
    Go to **Manage Content** in the sidebar. Wait until every source shows **Trained** before moving on.
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/manage-content.png" alt="Manage Content screen showing trained sources with green status indicators" />
    </Frame>
    Click on **View Content** icon on any trained source to see exactly what Asa extracted from it. If something looks incorrect, delete the source and retrain.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize to match your brand">
  Go to **Configuration** section in the sidebar. You can choose to customize **appearance** and **responses** to match your brand identity.

  <Tabs>
    <Tab title="Appearance">
      Set your avatar, brand color, widget position, assistant name, and other appearance attributes. The preview panel on the right updates in real time.

      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/customize-appearance.png" alt="Appearance settings showing avatar, theme, widget position, brand color and assistant name fields" />
      </Frame>

      <Tip>
        Set your avatar, brand color and assistant name. Everything else can be customized later on as per your requirements.
      </Tip>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Responses">
      Set your tone of voice, response length, and the handover method for where Asa hands shoppers off to when they need a real person support.

      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/customize-responses.png" alt="Responses settings showing tone of voice, response length and human support contact fields" />
      </Frame>

      <Tip>
        Need to customize responses further? Open the **Guidance** tab at the top of this section, or read [Guidance and custom controls](/guides/custom-guidance-controls).
      </Tip>
    </Tab>

  </Tabs>
  <Warning>To apply your changes, click **Save Changes** in the top right.</Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Deploy to your store">
  Now that Asa is trained your selected content and customized to match your brand identity, navigate to **Deploy** section via the sidebar and choose your channel to deploy Asa on.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/deploy-screen.png"
    alt="Deploy screen showing Shopify, HTML/JS, Headless and coming soon channel options"
  />
</Frame>

  <Tabs>
    <Tab title="Shopify">
    <Note>
      We're actively working on an Asa app for Shopify brands on the Shopify App Store. It is currently under review. Deployment gets much simpler than steps below once the app is live on the App Store. Stay up to date via our [changelog](/changelog).
    </Note>
      Click **Deploy** on the Shopify card. The following sidesheet opens with specific instructions. Follow it properly and in order to ensure correct deployment of Asa on your Shopify Storefront.

      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/deploy-shopify-screen.png" alt="Shopify deploy instructions screen" />
      </Frame>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Other Platforms">
      Click **Deploy** on other cards to find specific instructions to deploy Asa on:
      - HTML/CSS/JS Websites
      - NextJS (Headless Storefronts)
      - Whatsapp (Coming Soon)
      - Instagram Messaging (Coming Soon)
      - Voice (Coming Soon)
    </Tab>

  </Tabs>

<Tip>
  Need to test Asa before deploying it on your storefront? Access our playground
  view via the **Playground** tab in the **Deploy** section.
</Tip>

After you deploy, open your storefront and ask Asa a question as a shopper about one of your products or policies. You're live.

</Step>
</Steps>
