# Conversations

The **Conversations** section provides a detailed overview of every interaction between your customers and the AI Assistant.

Use it to monitor all conversations, identify where Asa needed help, and continuously refine your assistant’s responses, tone, and accuracy.

![Inbox](/images/inbox.png)

### View: All Conversations

All conversations are shown by default — giving you a complete view of every user interaction in one place.

### Filters

You can narrow down results using the filters at the top of the page:

- **Status Tabs:** Switch between high-level views of your inbox.
  - **All Conversations:** Shows every conversation for your assistant, with no status filter applied.
  - **Assisted:** Shows only conversations where Asa successfully resolved the user’s query.
  - **Redirected:** Shows only conversations where Asa handed the user over to your team.
- **Date Range:** Limit conversations to a specific start and end date so you can review activity for a chosen period (for example, last 1st Jan to 1st Feb).
- **Clear Date Filter:** Quickly remove the applied date range and go back to seeing conversations across all dates.

### Conversations Panel (Left)

This panel lists all recorded interactions with key details for each conversation:

- **Conversation Title:** A short summary of the conversation, helping you quickly recognise what it was about.
- **Status Badge:** Shows whether the conversation is currently open or closed, and whether it was _Assisted_ or _Redirected_.
- **Metadata Row:** Extra details about the session (such as end user and context) so you can understand who the conversation is with at a glance.
- **Relative Time:** Shows how long ago the conversation last had activity (for example, _2 mins ago_ or _3 days ago_).
- **Context Menu for Copy:** Right-click a conversation and choose **Copy Session ID** to copy the ID to your clipboard — share that ID with our support team if you need help or want to raise a ticket for that specific conversation.

### Messages Panel (Right)

When you click on a conversation, the detailed message history appears on the right side:

- **User Messages:** Messages sent by the customer, shown on one side of the chat.
- **Asa Responses:** Replies from your assistant, shown on the opposite side. These can include text and any rich content supported by Asa.
- **Empty State:** If a conversation has no messages yet, you will see a simple message explaining that there are no messages in this conversation.
- **Ticket Details:** When a conversation has been escalated, you can see extra ticket details beneath the message list to understand how and why it was handed over.

> **Note:** If you need assistance with any conversation, right-click the conversation in the left panel and select **Copy Session ID**, then share that ID with our team to raise a support request.
