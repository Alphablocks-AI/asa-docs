Asa uses outcome-based pricing: start free, upgrade when you need more conversations, knowledge sources, and intelligence. Every paid plan includes a **14-day free trial**. No setup fees or long-term contracts.

For live prices and the full comparison matrix, see [asa.so/pricing](https://asa.so/pricing).

<Info>
All prices are in **USD**. The website may show **Early Adopter** rates for Starter and Growth.
</Info>

## Plan overview

| Plan | Price (per month) | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Trying Asa on your store with no commitment |
| **Starter** | $49 | Stores ready to turn conversations into revenue |
| **Growth** | $199 | Brands that want full intelligence and visibility |

### Free

Try Asa on your store for free.

- **50** conversations / month
- **50** knowledge sources
- Shopify auto-training, files, URLs, snippets
- Google Drive and Notion integrations
- Self-serve support

### Starter

Everything in Free, plus:

- **500** conversations / month
- **250** knowledge sources
- Review integrations (Judge.me, Yotpo, Klaviyo Reviews)
- Advanced behavior, tone, and response controls
- Context-aware product guidance and personalized recommendations
- Reviews and AI summaries
- Basic upsell, cross-sell, and proactive nudges
- Session-only shopper memory
- Email support and immediate escalation notifications
- Basic conversation analytics, event tracking, and insights

### Growth

Everything in Starter, plus:

- **2,500** conversations / month
- **500** knowledge sources
- Advanced appearance customization
- Granular behavior, tone, and response controls
- Recommendations with past-purchase signals
- Advanced upsell, cross-sell, and proactive nudges
- Long-term shopper memory
- Priority email + Slack support
- Advanced inbox, analytics, event tracking, and business insights

## How to choose

- **Free** — Evaluate Asa, connect Shopify, and train a small catalog slice.
- **Starter** — You need review apps, stronger customization, nudges, and analytics as volume grows.
- **Growth** — You need higher limits, advanced nudges and insights, long-term shopper memory, and priority support.
