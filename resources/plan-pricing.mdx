### **Overview**

- Outcome-based pricing with no upfront costs — start free and upgrade as you grow.
- All paid plans include a **14-day free trial**.

## Free

- Best for evaluation and very small storefronts.
- **Included:**
  - 50 total conversations / month
  - 50 Knowledge Sources
  - Shopify, Notion, Google Drive integrations (no review apps)
  - GPT-4o-mini as default
  - Nudges of **Search** type and **Product Inactivity** type only

## Starter

- $19 / month — best for growing storefronts (~50K visitors/month).
- **Includes all Free features, plus:**
  - 500 total conversations / month
  - 250 Knowledge Sources
  - All integrations including review apps (Klaviyo, JudgeMe, Yotpo)
  - Advanced models as default
  - Advanced custom instructions and prompts (Guidance customization)
  - All basic Nudge types

## Growth

- $99 / month — best for scaling teams and higher-volume storefronts.
- **Includes all Starter features, plus:**
  - 2,500 total conversations / month
  - 500 Knowledge Sources
  - Advanced Nudge capabilities
  - HelpDesk (coming soon)

## Compare & Feature Highlights

- **Conversations (per month):** Free 50 → Starter 500 → Growth 2,500.
- **Knowledge Sources:** Free 50 → Starter 250 → Growth 500.
- **Integrations:** Free = Shopify, Notion, Google Drive → Starter & Growth = all integrations including review and marketing apps (Klaviyo, JudgeMe, Yotpo, etc.).
- **Models & Guidance:** Free uses GPT-4o-mini; Starter & Growth unlock advanced models and richer custom instructions/guidance.
- **Nudges:** Free gets Search + Product Inactivity nudges; Starter unlocks all current Nudge types; Growth adds advanced Nudge capabilities for more targeted engagement.
- **HelpDesk:** Available soon on Growth (and above).

## How to choose

- Pick **Free** to evaluate.
- Choose **Starter** if you expect ~50K monthly visitors, need higher conversation capacity, and want full integrations plus all Nudge types.
- Choose **Growth** if you handle higher volumes, need more Knowledge Sources, and want advanced Nudges and HelpDesk (when available).
